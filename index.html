Breathtaking Mountains

Older than the Himalayas mountains, the mountain chain of the Sahyadri represents geomorphic features of immense importance with unique biophysical and ecological processes. The combination of revisiting history amongst the splendid backdrop of hills, valleys, and forests of the region offers innumerable reasons to many fun-loving enthusiasts to explore its terrain.

River Streams

Rivers are the backbone of human civilizations which provide freshwater that is the basic necessity for human life. All civilizations in the past and present were born near river banks.Many rivers of Maharashtra have been revered in Hindu scriptures for many millennia and continue to harbor and nourish a rich cultural heritage.During the monsoon, Sahyadri ranges become the origin of many perennial river systems of Maharashtra like Godavari,Kaveri,Krishna & Tungabhadra and all of them are rain-fed.

Sacred Temples

Sahyadri is aptly translated and described as the Benevolent Mountains,are massive and ancient mountain ranges,that protect and nurture peninsular India.Many ancient temples are located in the midst of gigantic Sahyadri mountains. The forest temples in Maharashtra, nestled in their fastness are perfect examples of biodiversity preserved for ages as a part of popular culture.There are over 100 Tirthas and 80 Kshetras in the Sahyadri range.
